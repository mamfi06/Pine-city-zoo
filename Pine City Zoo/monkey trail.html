MONKEY TRAIL

Welcome to the Monkey Trail!

Swing into action and test your agility, balance, and monkey smarts!

Safety First

Rules and guidelines to keep every explorer safe while having fun.

  1. Log Balance Walk - Keep your balance like a macaque on a tree branch.
  2. Rope Climb Wall - Climb high like a spider monkey in the canopy.
  3. Wobbly Bridge - Cross carefully, just like monkeys crossing vines.
  4. Jungle Vine Swing - Swing across “rivers” like a gibbon in the wild.
  5. Net Tunnel Crawl - Crawl and climb through a monkey maze.
  6. Leap Like a Lemur - Test your jumping skills with ground-to-platform leaps!
