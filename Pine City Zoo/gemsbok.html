GEMSBOK

The gemsbok, also known as the oryx, is a large antelope native to arid regions of Southern Africa, such as the Kalahari Desert.

It has a striking appearance with long, straight horns (up to 4 feet long in both males and females), a grayish-tan body, black-and-white facial markings, and black stripes on its legs and flanks.
